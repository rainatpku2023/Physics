#### 方程非齐次项按照球谐函数展开
+ 考虑定解问题：

$ \nabla^2 u = v(r,\theta,\phi) $

$ u|_{r=a} = 0 $

+ 首先对非齐次项按照球谐函数进行本征函数展开：

$ v(r,\theta,\phi) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k} (\rho _{kn1}(r)\sin n \phi + \rho_{kn2}(r) \cos n\phi) P_k^n(\cos \theta)  $

+ 或者写为：

$ v(r,\theta,\phi) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k} \rho _{kn1}(r)S_{kn1}(\theta,\phi)+\rho _{kn2}(r)S_{kn2}(\theta,\phi) $

#### 设定一般解
+ 假设满足题意的一般解$ u_0 $：

$ u_0 =\sum_{l=0}^{\infin}\sum_{m=0}^{l} R _{lm1}(r)S_{lm1}(\theta,\phi)+R _{lm2}(r)S_{lm2}(\theta,\phi) $

+ 下面的问题是如何确定球谐函数的叠加系数，即$ R_{lm1},R_{lm2} $的形式。
    - <font style="color:#DF2A3F;">此处需要注意：</font>$ \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial R_{lm1}}{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial }{\partial r}) - \frac{l(l+1)}{r^2} $<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">不一定为零，因为假如为零那么方程的非齐次项就也为零了。</font>
+ $ S_{lm1},S_{lm2} $<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">是标准的球谐函数，因为这是本征函数。</font>

####  拉普拉斯算子径向分量和角向分量的分离
+ 考虑到：

$ \nabla^2  = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial }{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial }{\partial r})+ \frac{1}{r^2 \sin \theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}(\sin\theta \frac{\partial  }{\partial \theta}) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta} \frac{\partial^2}{\partial \phi^2} $

+ 定义：



$ \nabla^2_r  = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial }{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial }{\partial r}) $

$ \nabla^2_{ang} =  \frac{1}{r^2 \sin \theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}(\sin\theta \frac{\partial  }{\partial \theta}) + \frac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta} \frac{\partial^2}{\partial \phi^2} $

+ 那么：

$ \nabla^2 u_0 =
(\nabla^2_r +\nabla^2_{ang}) u_0  \\
=(\nabla^2_r +\nabla^2_{ang})\sum_{l=0}^{\infin}\sum_{m=0}^{l} R _{lm1}(r)S_{lm1}(\theta,\phi)+R _{lm2}(r)S_{lm2}(\theta,\phi)  \\
 $

+ 因为$ R_{lm1},R_{lm2} $与$ \theta,\phi $无关，$ S_{lm1},S_{lm2} $与$ r $无关：

$ \nabla^2 u_0=\sum_{l=0}^{\infin}\sum_{m=0}^{l} S_{lm1}\nabla^2_rR _{lm1}+S_{lm2}\nabla^2_rR _{lm2}+R_{lm1}\nabla_{ang}^2S_{lm1}+R_{lm2}\nabla_{ang}^2S_{lm2} $

+ 考虑到$ S_{lm1},S_{lm2} $<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">是标准的球谐函数</font>：

$ \nabla_{ang}^2S_{lm1} = -\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}S_{lm1} $

$ \nabla_{ang}^2S_{lm2} = -\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}S_{lm2} $

    - 并且注意到：



$ \nabla_{r}^2R_{lm1} \neq l(l+1){r^2}R_{lm1} $

$ \nabla_{r}^2R_{lm2} \neq l(l+1){r^2}R_{lm2} $

+ 可以知道：

$ \nabla^2 u_0=
\sum_{l=0}^{\infin}\sum_{m=0}^{l} S_{lm1}(\nabla^2_rR _{lm1}-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm1})+S_{lm2}(\nabla^2_rR _{lm2}-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm2}) $

+ 考虑到：

$ \nabla^2 u = v(r,\theta,\phi) = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k} \rho _{kn1}(r)S_{kn1}(\theta,\phi)+\rho _{kn2}(r)S_{kn2}(\theta,\phi) $

#### 比较系数，列出径向待定系数方程并求解
+ 根据本征函数正交性：

$ \nabla^2_rR _{lm1}-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm1} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k}\rho_{kn1}(r) \delta_{lk}\delta_{mn} $

$ \nabla^2_rR _{lm2}-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm2} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k}\rho_{kn2}(r) \delta_{lk}\delta_{mn} $

+ 代入$ \nabla^2_r  = \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial }{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial }{\partial r}) $，考虑定解条件$ u|_{r=a} = 0 $：
    - 得到$ R_{lm1},R_{lm2} $相应的定解问题：

$ \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial }{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial R _{lm1} }{\partial r})-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm1} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k}\rho_{kn1}(r) \delta_{lk}\delta_{mn} $

$ R _{lm1}(0)有界; \;R_{lm1}(a) =0 $



$ \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial }{\partial r}(r^2 \frac{\partial R _{lm2} }{\partial r})-\frac{l(l+1)}{r^2}R _{lm2} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infin}\sum_{n=0}^{k}\rho_{kn2}(r) \delta_{lk}\delta_{mn} $

$ R _{lm2}(0)有界; \;R_{lm2}(a) =0 $

#### 根据叠加系数确定原来问题的通解
+ 求出来$ R_{lm1},R_{lm2} $的表达式，代入就得到原来定解问题的解：

$ u =\sum_{l=0}^{\infin}\sum_{m=0}^{l} R _{lm1}(r)S_{lm1}(\theta,\phi)+R _{lm2}(r)S_{lm2}(\theta,\phi) $
